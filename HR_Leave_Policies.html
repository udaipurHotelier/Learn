Rules for Granting Leaves

  • Leave requests must be submitted in advance unless it's an emergency.
  • All leaves must be approved by the reporting manager.
  • Sick leave requires a valid medical certificate if exceeding two days.
  • Unused leave may be carried forward as per company policy.
  • Unauthorized absence may result in disciplinary action.

Encashment Rules

  • Employees may encash unused paid leave as per company policy.
  • Encashment is usually applicable at the end of the financial year.
  • Encashment is calculated based on basic salary and tenure.
  • Sick leave and casual leave are generally not encashable.

How to Apply for Leave

To apply for leave, employees should:

  • Submit a leave request via the company's HR portal or email.
  • Provide necessary documents (e.g., medical certificate for sick leave).
  • Get approval from the manager before taking leave.
  • Ensure timely submission to avoid work disruption.
